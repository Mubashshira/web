    About Us

    The Coding Junior is one of the upcoming venture in the world of coding nurturing. It is a leading children institute for self empowerment. Having it's office in Kanpur - the commercial hub of Uttar Pradesh and home to a leading technical institute - the IIT Kanpur, TCJ The Coding Junior has been the brainchild of young Tech Graduates who wanted to take coding essentials learning to be imparted the way it should be with focus on coding essentials, first. The Manpower behind this Social objective of empowering kids the correct way, are themselves the Power Programmers hired by Reputed IT firm Infosys. They have come ahead to also take this as a entrepreneur model. The founders include a mix of young & experienced tech people, researchers and E Commerce professionals with a decade of national and international experience into their respective fields.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to empower the children starting from 6 year kids to 15 year teens to be the future ready leaders in the ever-changing fast e-tech scenario across the world.

    Our Goal

    Our goal is to form an innovative society where every kids believes in innovation and solves real life problems.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to initiate and develop the creativity skill set in each child for programming, to make them future leaders and problem solvers.

    What Your Kid can Learn
